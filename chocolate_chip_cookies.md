# Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients

* 3/4 Cup Sugar
* 3/4 Cup Brown Sugar
* 1 Cup Shortening
* 2 Eggs
* 1 Teaspoon Vanilla
* 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
* 1 Teaspoon Salt
* 2 Cups Flour
* Chocolate Chips
* Oatmeal (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix sugars and shortening.
2. Mix in eggs, vanilla, baking soda, and salt.
3. Mix in flour.
4. Add chocolate chips and oatmeal
5. Bake at 375 degrees for 9-10 minutes
